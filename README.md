## 🚀 Featured Project

### 🥗 NutriOptimizer
> *An AI-integrated nutrition intelligence application*

NutriOptimizer uses AI to help users track their **daily caloric intake**, understand their **nutritional profile**, and receive **personalized recommendations** to achieve their digestion and nutrition goals.

**Key Features:**
- 🔢 Daily calorie calculation based on user profile & activity level
- 🎯 Goal-based nutrition planning (weight loss, maintenance, muscle gain)
- 💡 AI-powered suggestions for optimal digestion and nutrient absorption
- 📈 Progress tracking and adaptive recommendations
